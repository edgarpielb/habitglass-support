HabitGlass includes a Vacation Mode feature! You can pause individual habits during your vacation, and your streaks will be protected. Go to any habit's settings and use "Pause Habit" to set a vacation period. Your streak will resume when you return.

HabitGlass offers multiple backup options: (1) iCloud Sync automatically backs up to iCloud, (2) Automatic iCloud Drive Backup creates periodic backups you can access from Files app, (3) Manual Export lets you create a backup file anytime from Settings ‚Üí Data ‚Üí Export Backup.
